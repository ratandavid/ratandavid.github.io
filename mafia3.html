Mafia III

Mafia III has been developed under the banner of Hangar 13 and is published by 2K. This game was released on 7th October, 2016.
Mafia III is about Lincoln Clay who after returning from the Vietnam War, is back to his home in New Bordeaux where he is struggling to escape from his criminal past. But after he came to know that his surrogate family has been trashed and wiped away by the Italian Mafia, he once again reunite the ones left and starts a military like revenge from the ones who are behind this chaos.

Following are the main features of Mafia III that you will be able to experience after the first install on your Operating System.
