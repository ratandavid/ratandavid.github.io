Mass Effect Andromeda PC

Mass Effect Andromeda has been developed by BioWare and is published under the banner of Electronic Arts. This game was released on 21st March, 2017. This game is the 4th entry in the world acclaimed Mass Effect series.

Mass Effect Andromeda has been set in 22nd century where humans are planning to populate other planets. This game revolves around Sara or Scot Ryder, an inexperienced military recruit who joins Initiative. You need to take control Scott or Sara Ryder from 3rd person perspective. You are required to explore the Andromeda galaxy by selecting destinations from a ship known as Tempest. You can travel through different planets on a six-wheeled named Nomad. Mass Effect Andromeda has got some staggering visuals and the sound effects are amazing as well.
