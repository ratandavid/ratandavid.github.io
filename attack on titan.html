Attack On Titan 2 PC Game

Attack On Titan 2 also know as A.O.T 2 is a Japanese game developed by Omega Force and above all published by Koei Tecmo. It is an action game released in March 2018 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation, Xbox and above all for Nintendo Switch.

Attack On Titan 2 is based on the Hajime Isayama’s manga. The game covers the more than 50 chapters and above all giving the player freedom to create his character. A story mode is introduced to make the game near to the manga. Developer of the game use cell shaded graphics and give player access to defeat the titans in large area.

Features of Attack on Titan

One of the new features revealed are the Buddy Actions; these are support options that team members can provide to each other and include Rescue, Recovery and Titan Transformation which is a skill that offers the ability to unlock a Titan ability.
